Workflow

Today’s exercise is more free-form than some of the others. Your goal is to explore the leaps package and examine some model selection criteria. You will

  1. Fit the full model using lm.
  2. Choose a model with both Cp and BIC by forward selection.
  3. Choose a model with both Cp and BIC by backward selection.
  4. WE WON’T choose a model with both Cp and BIC by best-subset selection. This is too slow.
  5. Compare the Loo CV scores among the 5 models.

Doing CV on them

Unfortunately, the regsubsets objects don’t work easily with our CV formula.

Recall what the hat matrix is. And the values we need.

new_cv <- function(regss_obj, full, y, id=NULL){
  y = y[-full$na.action] # get rid of missing values
